Our Story

The House Behind the Jewels

Every piece at House of Talat is born from a belief — that a woman deserves to feel like royalty, not just on her wedding day, but every time she puts on something beautiful.

We create heirloom jewellery that sits at the intersection of Mughal heritage and modern elegance. Each earring is handcrafted with kundan, meenakari, polki, and precious stones — techniques passed down through generations of master artisans. Bold. Timeless. Iconic. Made to be worn, cherished, and passed on.
